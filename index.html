ABOUT ME


Hello there, I'm Kayla Lei. I am a web developer, an artist, and a creater. Born and raised in Rhode Island, I have been drawing almost all of my life. I like to focus primarily on traditional illustrations, using mediums such as ink and watercolor, but I also dabble in the digital arts and would like to learn more about web design.
I am currently challenging myself to draw something everyday for a year to help increase my creativity and keep me motivated.

I hope to be able to connect my artistic side with my web development skills.
